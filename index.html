Three Favourite Places in Kharagpur

Kharagpur Lake


Kharagpur Lake is a beautiful and alluring lake, and it is also one of the most popular locations in the city for people looking for an escape into nature. Moreover, this lake is surrounded by lush greenery.

Bhimbandh Wildlife Santuary


The Bhimbandh Wildlife Sanctuary is located amid the Kharagpur Hills, and it is home to a wide variety of exotic flora and animals.

Rameshwar Kund


Rameshwar Kund can be found in close proximity to Kharagpur Lake. It is a magnificent picnic location that is not to be missed, particularly during the winter.
